## 3. Professional LinkedIn Version (English)

ARBCore SwiftConnect is now ready for controlled **Enterprise Beta** testing.

It is a CRM and WhatsApp workflow platform designed to help businesses manage contacts, organize customer communication, prepare auto-reply rules, and view operational insights from one dashboard.

The beta version focuses on production-safe behavior. It does not fake WhatsApp sending. Real outbound delivery requires a properly configured WhatsApp Cloud API setup, and the app only reports send success after provider confirmation.

Current beta capabilities include contacts management, auto-reply rule management, settings persistence, team management, dashboard statistics, safe message-attempt handling, and clear license/beta status.

This is not being positioned as a fully complete enterprise SaaS yet. Billing enforcement, complete webhook automation, advanced campaign scheduling, and deeper analytics are planned for later phases.

Interested business owners and teams are welcome to request a demo and share early feedback.
